Your Safety In Your Hands

Are you worried about your son/daughter returning late from office or going out for late-night parties? Concerned about not being able to take midnight strolls all by yourself? Worry not, Amigo has got something for you.
Amigo is a safety alert app developed by students from the Indian Institute of Technology. Amigo acts as the one-stop platform at the time of emergency. You need to press the HELP button three long times following which:
1. App users within 5 km distance and the user's contacts will receive an alert notification, which will notify them about the impending danger.
2. A group of the app users within 5 km and the user's favorite contacts is formed where they could discuss the possible ways of reaching and helping the user.
3. The user's live location details and personal details(name, age, profile pic, for the ease of identification) are displayed in all the groups of the user and the group of the nearby users formed.
4. The user can send voice and video messages in a hassle-free manner, which will be sent to all the groups.
5. The user can search for nearby police station locations and their contact numbers as well as routes to safer places in one click.
Always test run the app with your family members to understand it's working. Add your dear ones (family members, friends, relatives) to the app, send them friend requests, add them to your contacts, form their groups, choose favorite contacts and test run the app.
